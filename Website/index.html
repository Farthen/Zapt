Zapt for Trakt

Zapt is an iOS application made for Trakt.tv, a webservice for managing your movies and TV shows. You can add and remove content to/from your watchlists, manage your library, rate, check in, mark as seen and much more.

AVAILABLE SOON

Use everywhere

You have access to your last watched shows on the go. It also displays the next episode to watch, if there is one. This makes it super easy to use for watching episode after episode and checking in into all of them.

Caching

Zapt caches all requests made to the website and thus shows you the content you want immediately. It then updates the content in the background with new data if such data is available. This makes Zapt super fast to use.
